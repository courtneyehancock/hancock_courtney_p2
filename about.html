About Me

I survive off a Sirius love for Harry Potter, dogs, dancing and a dependable diet of Coca-Cola and Frosted Flakes. I have an appreciation for puns, carbohydrates and, according to a Buzzfeed quiz on Facebook, the color of my energy is yellow.

I attend the University of Florida in the College of Journalism and Communications as a public relations major, with a concentration and interest in history. I am a visual communicator and have a love for creation, so I am currently in the combined degree program to obtain my masters of mass communication in web design and online communication.

Upon graduation I hope to work for an animal protection nonprofit through the public relations department to help promote animal abuse awareness and advocate love for all things living.
