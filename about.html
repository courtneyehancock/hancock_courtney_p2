About Me

I survive off a Sirius love for Harry Potter, dogs, dancing and a dependable diet of Coca-Cola and Frosted Flakes. I have an appreciation for puns, carbohydrates and, according to a Buzzfeed quiz on Facebook, the color of my energy is yellow.

I earned my Bachelor of Science in Public Relations with a concentration and minor in History from the University of Florida in Fall 2018. I am currently a graduate student within the UF College of Journalism and Communications working on my MAMC in Web Design and Online Communication.

My ambition is to work for an animal protection nonprofit through the public relations department and help promote awareness of animal abuse and advocate love for all things living.
